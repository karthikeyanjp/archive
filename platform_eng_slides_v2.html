Partnership Expectations

Building Together: Collaborative Partnership Model for Platform Success

Platform-First Development Principles

Ensuring architectural consistency, reducing technical debt, and accelerating delivery

1
Architecture Validation Partnership
Engage with our team early to vet and validate architectural decisions before implementation. This ensures alignment with platform standards and prevents costly rework down the line.
Business Impact
Prevents architectural misalignment and reduces rework cycles by 80%, saving 6-8 weeks per major initiative
2
Modern Stack Adoption
Commit to using modern technologies and frameworks provided by the platform team. Legacy technology choices should be avoided to maintain consistency, security, and long-term maintainability.
Business Impact
Standardizes technology stack, improves team mobility, and reduces skill gap training costs by 50%
3
Shared Solution Awareness
No reusable solutions should be built in isolation. All potentially shared components must be developed with platform team awareness to prevent duplication and maximize organizational benefit.
Business Impact
Maximizes component reuse across teams, prevents "not invented here" syndrome, accelerates feature delivery
4
Unified Team Approach
Work as one integrated team rather than separate entities. This collaborative model ensures shared ownership, knowledge transfer, and collective success in delivering healthcare solutions.
Business Impact
Breaks down silos, improves knowledge sharing, and creates unified ownership of platform success
5
Strategic Architecture Inclusion
Include platform engineering in all strategic discussions related to architecture and platform decisions. Our expertise ensures technical decisions align with organizational goals and platform evolution.
Business Impact
Ensures technology roadmap alignment with business strategy and prevents costly architectural pivots
