Partnership Expectations

Building Together: Collaborative Partnership Model for Platform Success

1
Architecture Validation Partnership: Engage with our team early to vet and validate architectural decisions before implementation. This ensures alignment with platform standards and prevents costly rework down the line.
2
Modern Stack Adoption: Commit to using modern technologies and frameworks provided by the platform team. Legacy technology choices should be avoided to maintain consistency, security, and long-term maintainability.
3
Shared Solution Awareness: No reusable solutions should be built in isolation. All potentially shared components must be developed with platform team awareness to prevent duplication and maximize organizational benefit.
4
Unified Team Approach: Work as one integrated team rather than separate entities. This collaborative model ensures shared ownership, knowledge transfer, and collective success in delivering healthcare solutions.
5
Strategic Architecture Inclusion: Include platform engineering in all strategic discussions related to architecture and platform decisions. Our expertise ensures technical decisions align with organizational goals and platform evolution.
