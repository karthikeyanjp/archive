Platform-First Development Principles

Ensuring architectural consistency, reducing technical debt, and accelerating delivery

1
Architecture Design Review (ADR) Process
Mandatory platform team review for all new services, data models, and integration patterns before development sprint planning.
Business Impact
Reduces post-deployment issues by 75% and prevents technical debt accumulation
2
Modern Stack Standardization
All new development must use platform-approved technology stack. Legacy system integrations require migration roadmap approval.
Business Impact
Reduces maintenance costs by 40% and improves security posture compliance
3
Shared Component Governance
Platform team maintains registry of reusable components. All potentially shared functionality requires platform consultation during design phase.
Business Impact
Prevents duplicate development effort, saving 200+ engineering hours per quarter
4
Embedded Platform Engineering
Platform engineers participate in product team ceremonies. Product teams assign technical leads for platform collaboration and knowledge transfer.
Business Impact
Improves deployment success rate to 98% and reduces rollback incidents by 60%
5
Strategic Technology Roadmap
Platform team participates in quarterly business planning, technology investment decisions, and vendor evaluation processes.
Business Impact
Ensures technology decisions align with scalability requirements and compliance needs
6
Platform SLA & Support Model
99.9% uptime SLA for platform services with 24/7 on-call support. Dedicated Slack channels and documented escalation procedures.
Business Impact
Guarantees platform reliability with <4 hour incident resolution time
